Privacy Policy

Saarthi Technologies — Chrome Extensions

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the following Chrome extensions published by Saarthi Technologies:

Central Govt Payroll Manager

7th CPC payroll management for Central Government offices.

Inventory Management Pro

Full-featured inventory management system for businesses.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Both extensions operate entirely offline on your device. All data you enter — employee records, salary information, inventory items, invoices, etc. — is stored locally using the chrome.storage API and SQLite WASM (bundled with the extension). No data ever leaves your browser.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personally identifiable information is transmitted
  • No health information
  • No financial or payment information is transmitted
  • No authentication information (passwords, credentials)
  • No location data
  • No web history or browsing activity
  • No personal communications
  • No website content

Permissions Used

Central Govt Payroll Manager

  • storage — Saves payroll data (employees, salary records, settings) locally on your device.
  • unlimitedStorage — Required because payroll data can exceed the standard storage quota.
  • offscreen — Generates PDF documents (salary slips, Form 16, pension slips) in the background without interrupting your workflow.
  • host_permissions (https://ifsc.razorpay.com/*) — Fetches bank IFSC code details to validate bank information on salary/bank slips. Only the IFSC code you enter is sent; no personal data is shared.
  • wasm-unsafe-eval (CSP) — Required by the bundled SQLite WASM library for offline database operations. No remote code is executed.

Inventory Management Pro

  • storage — Saves inventory data (items, categories, purchases, sales, invoices) locally on your device.
  • unlimitedStorage — Required for large inventory datasets.
  • offscreen — Generates PDF invoices and reports in the background.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties. The only external network request made by our extensions is to the Razorpay IFSC API (in Payroll Manager) to look up bank branch details — and this only contains the public IFSC code you enter, not any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our extensions are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of the extensions after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
